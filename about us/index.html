Meet Our CEO

Michael Ayodeji Oliyide is the visionary founder and CEO of Mikky Technology. He is a skilled web developer and digital solutions expert with a passion for turning ideas into scalable realities.

Michael began his academic journey at Deril Academy in Ibadan, Oyo State. He expanded his skills at SQI - College of ICT and is currently expanding his journey at Lead City University, Ibadan where he continues to grow his technical and leadership capabilities.

His innovative mindset, dedication to excellence, and strong vision drive Mikky Technology's success in providing cutting-edge digital services for individuals, startups, and organizations.

"Technology should empower, not complicate — let's build smarter."
